about

I started my Computer Science journey in 2020, at the time of pandemic. I was always curious how can a piece of metal which is non-living(our PC’s CPU) can do logic ?

I started reading about computer’s history back in 2020 and opted for Bachelor’s of Computer Applications in 2021. I started with some low-level languages like Assembly, C but wasn’t getting any working application out of them, then I suddenly stumbled upon many amazing animated websites and got into creative web development, where I created many Web Applications, Creative Websites for brands and also worked on a Web3 Platform with React.js.

I now have 3+ years of experience and I am currently working at Pallavi Nopany Design Studio as Lead Developer, where I build Client Projects and lead junior developers of other agencies in creative development.

In the meantime I learn and do Low-Level programming and also study backend development regularly to become a full-stack developer. I am still in the final year of my degree and I am open to more exciting opportunities.
